Professional Profile

I am a Technical Team Leader for IBM Global Business Services in the Government Sector working on a Social Services project in Olympia, WA. I have behind me more than twenty-six years of experience in mainframe applications development, mainframe application maintenance, and end-user support.

I currently function as the Development Team Leader for a Data Collection team. This role provides direction, instructions and guidance to the application team for their deliverables, day to day operations and application maintenance. This role is accountable for the team's deliverables (Problem Report solutions, Change Requests and Projects) across all stages of the product life cycle while ensuring on-time and in-budget delivery of quality code.

Prior to my current role, I was responsible for maintaining a series of data collection applications as well as system support activities at a customer location. I am able to work independently or in a team environment, throughout all phases of a project’s life cycle.
